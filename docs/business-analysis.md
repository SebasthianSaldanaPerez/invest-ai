# Business Analysis

## Business Domains

InvestAI is divided into two main business domains.

### Market Intelligence

Responsible for collecting and analyzing information about publicly traded companies.

Includes:

- Company
- Stock
- Price History
- Financial Statements
- News
- AI Analysis
- Opportunity Score

Its objective is to help the investor understand a company before investing.

---

### Personal Investment Intelligence

Responsible for analyzing the investor's own behavior.

Includes:

- Investment Operations
- Watchlist
- Performance
- Statistics
- Machine Learning
- Personalized Insights

Its objective is to identify patterns in the investor's historical decisions.
