# Domain Model

## Core Entities

### Company

Represents the business behind a publicly traded stock.

---

### Stock

Represents a tradable financial instrument identified by a ticker symbol.

---

### Price History

Stores historical market prices for a stock.

---

### Financial Statement

Represents a company's financial reports.

---

### News

Stores news articles related to a company.

---

### Investment Operation

Represents a buy/sell decision made by the user.

---

### Watchlist

Stores stocks the user wants to monitor.

---

### Opportunity Score

Represents the calculated investment opportunity based on user-defined rules.

---

### AI Analysis

Stores AI-generated summaries and explanations.
